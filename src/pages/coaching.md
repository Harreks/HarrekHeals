# Coaching

My primary goal is helping people, but as time is limited I inevitably have to charge for it at some point. I don't want money to get in the way of people getting help so even if you can't or don't want to pay for it please message me anyway if you want me to take a look at something, I am always happy to chat and assist in whatever way possible.

I do written reviews via discord DMs or calls where we go over material to help you solve your issues. It can cover from the very basics of starting as a healer to high level optimizations for Preservation Evoker or Restoration Shaman, including some niche topics like UI, macros, and external tools.

If you are looking for help with a spec I don't personally cover I will be happy to refer you to high level players that are also my friends and will be happy to help you.

# Pricing

For everything that is done via written text you can assume a <a>$10 USD cost per session</a>, calls or videos would go up to <a>$40 USD per session</a>. I prefer to list these as *per session* rather than *per hour* because i don't want complex issues to go unresolved for the sake of taking less time. If we sit down to review your gameplay we will go until all your questions are answered, within reason.

I am flexible about pricing and my main goal is always to make sure people are getting help. So if you don't think these prices are correct feel free to contact me and we can discuss it.
